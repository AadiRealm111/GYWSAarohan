MT Donation Drive

Under the initiative AAROHAN, students of Mother Teresa Hall of Residence, IIT Kharagpur distributed notebooks, stationery, and food items to the children of JVM on the eve of Children's Day.
The Hall President of Mother Teresa Hall of Residence stated that they had conducted a "Children's Day Donation Drive" in their hostel in which all the boarders came forward and came forward and showed their immense interest in helping the needy.

Adventure Sports

Adventure Activities for the students of JVM in asscoiation with Technology Adeventure Society(TaDS) was held in IIT Kharagpur near Technology Students' Gymkhana. Multiple activities for the students were conducted which includes zorbing, archery and jumping jacks. It was a good extra curricualr activity for the students of JVM. Also, we successfully created a venue to invlove students of IIT Kharagpur in some social activities.
